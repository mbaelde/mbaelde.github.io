I'm a Senior Data Scientist, with some experiences in digital audio processing, retail and digital marketing. I manage projects from start to finish, mainly: data analysis, model experimentation, training and serving.

My Ph.D. thesis was focused on real-time audio source localization, identification and separation. My supervisors were Christophe Biernacki (Scientific Deputy at [Inria Lille](https://www.inria.fr/en/)) and Raphaël Greff (R&D Director at [A-Volute](http://www.a-volute.com/)).

I graduated from [University Lille 1](https://www.univ-lille.fr/home/) (Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics, Master Research Applied Mathematics) and [Ecole Centrale de Lille](http://centralelille.fr/) (Master Decision and Data Analysis).

I'm also a self-taught musician (guitarist, bassist, pianist) for about 10 years, mainly playing Progressive Metal musics (Meshuggah, Periphery, ...) and Muse.
