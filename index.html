About me

I'm a Data Scientist, specialized in audio signal processing, generative models and Deep Learning.
My Ph. D thesis was focused on real-time audio source localization, identification and separation. My supervisors were Christophe Biernacki (Scientific Deputy at Inria Lille) and Raphaël Greff (R&D Director at A-Volute).
I'm graduated from University Lille 1 (Ph. D in Applied Mathematics, Master Research Applied Mathematics) and Ecole Centrale de Lille (Master Decision and Data Analysis).
I'm also a self-taught musician (guitarist, bassist, pianist) for about 10 years, mainly playing Progressive Metal musics (Meshuggah, Periphery, ...) and Muse. I have a YouTube channel where I post guitar and bass covers of various songs. I also have a Bandcamp page for my solo music project Fracton.
Finally I'm a casual video game player, Nintendaddict (The Legend of Zelda serie) and Switch player.
