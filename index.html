About me

I'm a Data Scientist and Ph. D Student, specialized in generative models and Deep Learning. My thesis is focused on real-time audio source localization, identification and separation. My Supervisors are Christophe Biernacki (Professor) and Raphaël Greff (A-Volute R&D Manager).

I'm graduated from Ecole Centrale de Lille (Master Decision and Data Analysis) and University Lille 1 (Master Research Applied Mathematics).
